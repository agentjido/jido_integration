    Jido.Integration.V2.ControlPlane (jido_integration v0.1.0)

    Copy Markdown

    Connector registry plus canonical run/attempt/event ledger.

    The control plane owns deterministic connector and capability discovery as well as the stable invocation boundary that powers the public facade.
